About the Game

You know that feeling when you'd spend forever stuck on one brutal Game Boy level? Well, we're totally bringing that back (sorry not sorry).

Super Matino 2: Adventure delivers classic side-scrolling gameplay with colorful environments, challenging enemies, and hidden secrets scattered across multiple worlds. Players guide Matino through dangerous levels filled with traps, collectibles, and powerful bosses.

Gameplay Highlights

Easy to pick up, but good luck putting it down

  • Jump Across Obstacles. Navigate moving platforms and dangerous terrain.

  • Defeat Bosses. Face larger enemies at the end of major stages.

  • Collect Coins & Rewards. Unlock bonuses and progression items.

  • Discover Hidden Areas. Explore beyond the main path for extra rewards.

  • Complete Challenging Levels. Difficulty increases naturally across worlds.

  • Unlock Power-Ups. Find special items to boost Matino's speed and abilities.
